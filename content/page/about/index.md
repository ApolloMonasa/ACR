Algorithm Contest Record (ACR) is my competitive programming notebook.

This site focuses on:

- Codeforces contest solutions and post-contest reviews
- AtCoder contest solutions and reflections
- Reusable patterns across problems (greedy, DP, graph, data structure, constructive ideas)

## Goal

Build a long-term, searchable record of solved contest problems and learned techniques.

## Notes

Content is updated continuously as new contests are completed.
